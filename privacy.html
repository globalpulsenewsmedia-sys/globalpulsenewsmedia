Privacy Policy

Global Pulse News is committed to protecting the data privacy of our readers and subscribers under global standards.

1. Principles of Data Collection

We process data in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and regional data protection statutes (including the India DPDPA). We collect:

  • Subscription Details: Email addresses collected during subscription signup to deliver newsletters or news updates.
  • Technical Metadata: Client IP addresses, browser agents, geolocation data, and page request counts. This data is collected strictly to support security configurations (DDoS mitigation) and optimize static asset routing.

2. Cookies & Telemetry (Cookiebot Integration)

We integrate Cookiebot to manage cookie consents interactively. Google Analytics (G-LX2P8H9BY4) is loaded conditionally based on your choices. Cookies are categorized into:

  • Necessary Cookies: Required to enable system navigation, consent preference storage, and dark-theme configurations.
  • Statistics & Analytics: Used to monitor anonymous reader traffic flows, regional load distributions, and page views.

3. Data Transfer & Storage

All collected telemetry and subscription logs are transmitted via SSL/TLS encryption and stored on secure cloud services. We do not sell, lease, or distribute email lists to external ad brokers or data warehouses. Data is only disclosed when required by standard legal warrant or standard compliance mandates.

4. User Rights under GDPR & DPDPA

As a user, you hold the following rights regarding your personal records:

  • Right of Access & Portability: Obtain exports of the email addresses and newsletter records associated with your account.
  • Right of Erasure: Request the deletion of your email address from our active subscription directories.
  • Right to Object: Oppose analytical cookie tracking using the interactive consent banner.

To submit a data request, please contact our Grievance Desk at contact@globalpulsenewsmedia.com.

5. Policy Revisions

We update this privacy policy periodically to remain aligned with regulatory revisions. The version date at the top of the page will update when edits are published.
